Hi there. My name is Mason.

I'm a student that likes learning about computers and technology. I find that understanding how computers can aid humans is vital in the progression of the species as a whole.

In particular, I'm interested in fields related to computational biology, physical simulations, language processing and understanding, network design, and chip design/architecture. Personally, in many of these areas, computers have great potential to evolve the way humans do work.

Most of the time, I write programs to try to make life easier or fill a small hole that I notice - somewhere that I think could use some improvements. From my first programs (writing a math quiz program to test my math skills) to the programs and apps I create now (mostly to automate tedious processes), I always aim to decipher how technology can improve the lives of people.
